# For listeners

Mirlo is a platform where you can discover music, support artists directly, and own your music collection.

## Discover and browse music

Explore music from independent artists and labels around the world. Browse artist profiles, listen to tracks and albums, and find new music through the Mirlo catalog.

## Purchase and download music

Buy music directly from artists and download it in the format you prefer. Your purchases are yours to keep — no subscriptions required to access music you've bought.

## Support artists with annual or monthly subscriptions

Want to support an artist on an ongoing basis? Subscribe to an artist's support tiers to help fund their work. Tiers can include perks like exclusive content, early access to releases, or subscriber-only blog posts.

Some artists offer free newsletters--you can subscribe to these even without a Mirlo account, so you can stay in the loop without committing to a paid subscription.

## Use promo codes

Have a promo code from an artist? Redeem it to get music for free. Artists use promo codes to share their work with press, friends, and fans. If you've got one, it's easy to use at checkout.

## Manage your collection

Keep track of everything you've purchased or downloaded from a single account. Your music library is always accessible and yours to re-download.

## Read artist posts

Follow along with what your favorite artists are up to. Many artists publish blog posts directly on their Mirlo profile—public updates or exclusive posts for subscribers only--and they'll land right in your inbox. You can see all updates from artists you follow in an easy to read notification feed on your profile page.

## No account required for some things

You can listen to previews and browse artist pages without an account. You only need to create one when you're ready to purchase, subscribe, or download.

## Follow artists from across the social web

Artists can choose to be followed on the social web.
