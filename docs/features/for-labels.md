# For labels

Label profiles are free to all users--a label profile gets all the benefits of an [artist profile](./for-artists) plus some more! See the [How To on label accounts](../how-tos/label-accounts.md) to get started.

## Roster

Showcase the entire roster of artists on your profile page.

## Managed artists

You can manage artist accounts you add as well as artists from other
users who give you permission to manage their artists.
